SM-Celltech is a gujarat based chemical company where you can purchase various chemical products. We offer a wide variety of products with excellent customer service.

To place an order, simply browse through our catalog, select your desired items, add them to the cart, and proceed to checkout. You will need to enter your shipping information and payment details to complete the order.

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also check the status of your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section.

We accept a variety of payment methods including credit/debit cards, paytm, and other secure payment gateways. You will be prompted to select your payment method during the checkout process.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. To return a product, simply visit the "Returns & Exchanges" section on our website and follow the instructions. You can initiate a return request within 30 days of receiving your order.

Once an order is confirmed, we are unable to modify it. However, you can contact our support team immediately after placing your order, and we will try to assist with cancellation or changes, depending on the order's status.
